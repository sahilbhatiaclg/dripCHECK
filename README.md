# 👕 DripCheck: AI Stylist for the Broke & Busy

### 🚀 The Notion
Let’s be real—college life is a mess. You’ve got a 9 AM lecture, a 2 PM internship, and a 9 PM party, but your closet is a pile of laundry on a chair. **DripCheck** is the AI stylist that turns your "laundry pile" into a "curated fit." No more mid-outfits. No more fashion crimes.

### 💎 The Vibe
- **Main Character Energy:** We don't just suggest clothes; we build an aesthetic.
- **Zero Effort:** Just snap a photo, and the AI does the heavy lifting.
- **Stay Humble:** Use the "Roast My Fit" feature when your ego gets too big for your sneakers.

### 🛠️ Cool Features (The "Drip" List)
* **Style Intelligence:** It’s not just "wear this"—it’s "wear this because the color theory is hitting."
* **Weather-Ready:** Automatically checks if you need a hoodie or if it's "short-kings" weather.
* **The Roast:** Brutally honest AI feedback. If the fit is a 0/10, we'll tell you.
* **Inspo Sync:** One-tap Pinterest search based on your actual closet.
